D-Day Count is a clean, modern, and completely privacy-focused countdown application designed to help you keep track of your most important life events and milestones. 

Whether you are counting down to an upcoming event or tracking the days that have passed since a memorable moment, D-Day Count offers a beautifully simple and seamless experience without any of the clutter.

Key Features:
• Clean & Minimalist UI: A gorgeous, distraction-free interface built with modern Android design standards.
• Countdowns & Count-ups: Easily track days remaining until a future event or count days elapsed since a past milestone.
• Privacy First: 100% Free and Open Source Software (FOSS). No tracking, no analytics, no unnecessary permissions, and absolutely no internet requirement. Your data stays securely on your device.
• Lightweight & Fast: Optimized to be gentle on your device resources and battery.

Take control of your important dates with a countdown app that truly respects your privacy.
